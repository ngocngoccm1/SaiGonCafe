The Vietnam Journey

A taste of memories.

Four chapters trace the memories, culture and flavours that shaped Sài Gòn Café.

01 · Origin

Where my journey began

Every dish carries a memory. My story began in Vietnam, from the old streets of Hanoi to the vibrant corners of Chợ Lớn, where family, childhood and the flavours of home shaped my love for Vietnamese food.

02 · Journey

From Vietnam to Gozo

After almost 40 years in Germany, a new chapter began. At 58, I followed a dream to bring the flavours, stories and street food spirit of Vietnam to the island of Gozo, creating a place where cultures meet.

03 · Philosophy

More than food, a memory to share

Vietnamese street food is about connection: simple ingredients, honest flavours and moments shared around a table. Every dish at Sài Gòn Café is inspired by the streets, markets and everyday life of Vietnam.

04 · Today

A little corner of Saigon in Gozo

Sài Gòn Café is a small home for Vietnamese flavours, where street food, traditional drinks and slow coffee come together, inviting everyone to experience a taste of Vietnam in the heart of Gozo.

Our Story · A Journey Through the Streets of Vietnam

The Vietnam Journey

Follow the streets that connect my beginnings, childhood memories and the spirit of Vietnamese street food with a new chapter in Gozo.

01 · Cat Linh Street · Hanoi

My Beginning

Every journey begins somewhere. Mine began on Cat Linh Street, Hanoi, a place where my first memories of Vietnam were created.

02 · Tran Tuong Cong Street · Chợ Lớn

My Childhood Memories

Family meals, the Chinese quarter, street vendors and humble everyday dishes shaped the flavours and memories I still carry with me.

03 · Saigon Alleys

The Soul of Street Food

Small alley kitchens, plastic stools, street vendors, the aroma of phin coffee and a hot bowl of phở in the morning: this is the soul of Sài Gòn Café.

04 · Nguyen Hue Street · Saigon

The Spirit of Saigon

Open, energetic and always bringing people together, Nguyễn Huệ connects the memories of the past with the rhythm of the present.

05 · Gozo · Malta

A New Chapter

After almost 40 years in Germany and a new beginning at 58, I chose Gozo as the place to share the flavours and memories of Vietnam.

Past becomes present

Old memories, a new home in Gozo.

From the streets of Vietnam to the island of Gozo, old memories are brought back to life through flavours, colours and stories. What began as childhood moments and family traditions now becomes a place where people gather, share and experience Vietnamese culture.

FreshnessIngredients full of colour, texture and life. Inspired by Vietnamese markets and street kitchens, every dish celebrates the natural flavours of fresh ingredients.
WarmthThe feeling of a familiar table. Like a meal shared at home, our food is created to bring comfort, connection and a sense of belonging.
ConnectionVietnamese flavours meeting the heart of Gozo. A bridge between two cultures, bringing a taste of Saigon to locals, travellers and everyone who walks through our doors.
